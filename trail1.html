Trail 1

I am interested in seeing if there is a correlation between life and financial satisfaction and what peoples think is important in life. My guess would be that the more money and better health, the less important family is and maybe leisure time is more important.

Started with selecting the wave. I took 1995-1998, 1999-2004, 2005-2009 and 2010-2014.

Next I choose all the possible countries. A lot of clicking!

Here I selected the variables I wanted to use.

This table shows important in life crossed with financial satisfaction.

It apperars that for people that are completely dissatisfied with their financial situation the family is very important.

It apperars that for people that are completely dissatisfied with their financial situation the leisure time is not as important as the family, which would confirm my theory.
